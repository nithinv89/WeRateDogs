Investigation Overview¶

The main goal of this project is to look into the various delay times within the 2007 Flight Dataset and find correlation with respect to the other variable

Dataset Overview¶

In this Project, we will be using Flights dataset taken from Stats_Computing. The Flights dataset essentially shows information of Flights from the US and includes data from 1987 to 2008. This rich data source contains so many variables for analysis including details pertaining to delays, flight information, causes of delays, taxi in/out, date etc. For my project, I am only including the analysis of the year 2007 since my system had touble encorporating all the data which surpasses to 1.5 GB.

Relation between Arrival Time Delay, Departure Time Delay and Elapsed Time Delay¶

In this visualization, we found the correlation between the three different delays and concluded a strong correlation between arrival and departure delay time. Elapsed time delay was not considered to be a strong enough correlation.

Line Plot comparing the various causes of delays in each month¶

Here we compare the various types of delays for every month of the year 2007. Here we can see that the 2 main reasons for Delays are the Weather and also the Late Arrival of the plane. It is also interesting to note the variation of these delays does differ month to month drastically.

Carrier_month = flights_2007.query('CarrierDelay > 0').groupby('Month').agg({'CarrierDelay':'mean'})
Weather_month = flights_2007.query('WeatherDelay > 0').groupby('Month').agg({'WeatherDelay':'mean'})
NASDelay_month = flights_2007.query('NASDelay > 0').groupby('Month').agg({'NASDelay':'mean'})
Security_month = flights_2007.query('SecurityDelay > 0').groupby('Month').agg({'SecurityDelay':'mean'})
LateAircraft_month = flights_2007.query('LateAircraftDelay >0').groupby('Month').agg({'LateAircraftDelay':'mean'})

plt.figure(figsize=[20,10])

plt.errorbar(x = Carrier_month.index, y = Carrier_month['CarrierDelay'])
plt.errorbar(x = Weather_month.index, y = Weather_month['WeatherDelay'])
plt.errorbar(x = NASDelay_month.index, y = NASDelay_month['NASDelay'])
plt.errorbar(x = Security_month.index, y = Security_month['SecurityDelay'])
plt.errorbar(x = LateAircraft_month.index, y = LateAircraft_month['LateAircraftDelay'])

plt.xlabel('Month')
plt.ylabel('Delay Time in Minutes')
plt.title('Month vs Delay Time Due to Carrier, Weather, NAS, Security or Late Aircraft')
plt.legend(['Carrier Delay', 'Weather Delay', 'NAS Delay', 'Security Delay', 'Late Aircraft Delay'])

plt.show()
